How I work

Three principles.

01
Architect first.
I design the logic before writing a single line. The Projection Planner covering 12 brands and 8 platforms started as a whiteboard — inputs, outputs, failure modes — before a single line of Python was written.
02
AI as execution layer.
I use Claude, ChatGPT, and Copilot as the execution layer, not the thinking layer. This is how I reduced forecast generation from hours to 3 seconds — without a dedicated engineering team.
03
Decisions over dashboards.
The unified Power BI dashboard across 8 platforms was not built to display numbers. It was built so budget reallocation decisions could happen in real time — not next week.
